About

SHE:KON/GREETINGS.

Eva Bowering is an Indigiqueer entrepreneur and advocate from mixed Settler and Haudenosaunee (Kanien’kéhaka Kahnawake-Akwesasne) heritage. Born in "Niagara", Eva's journey includes navigating poverty, reconnection after forced assimilation and intergenerational trauma, and systemic challenges through urban Indigeneity. With a background in social services and a diploma in Community and Developmental Support Work, Eva specializes in peer support, harm reduction, trauma-informed practices, disability justice, radical mental health, grief work, and promoting Indigenous principles and anti-colonial perspectives in consulting and coaching. Their work integrates wellness, empowerment, and cultural revitalization in Indigenous communities and beyond.
